Welcome to Instoo!



Instoo works by interacting with real people, which sends notifications to their phones like advertisements. First, pick targets related to your account:

Target Hashtags


Set The Language At Instagram.com to English

Our new targeting algorithm requires the language at Instagram.com to be set to english.

Please check now before starting the bot


1) Click your profile picture in the top right corner, then click instagram-user-profileProfile.

2) Click Edit Profile.

3) Click Language at the very bottom of the page and select a new language.


Enable Likes, Follows, Stories Daily

Instoo runs in background tabs and will auto-shut off after 10 hours.

You only need to turn Instoo on once a day to get constant daily growth.

The sliders are upper limits, but Instoo will auto-adjust speeds dynamically for each user.

Make sure the bot does at least 100 likes and 100 follows a day.

Keep the Instoo tab and the Instagam tab open. Don't allow your PC/laptop to auto-sleep.

Disable javascript throttling in chrome://flags to allow Instoo to run fast in the background.
